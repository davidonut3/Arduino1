==== pin layout knoppen ====
De pinnen van links naar rechts:
pin  1 - not connected
pin  2 - knop 'source'
pin  3 - knop 'right'
pin  4 - knop 'left'
pin  5 - knop 'menu'
pin  6 - GND
pin  7 - led rood
pin  8 - led groen
pin  9 - knop 'power'

NIET AANGESLOTEN:

pin 10 - GND
pin 11 - not connected
pin 12 - not connected

Grote connector:

GND - pin 6
D2  - pin 9
D3  - pin 5
D4  - pin 4
D5  - pin 8 (LED PWM)
D6  - pin 7 (LED PWM)
D7  - pin 3
D8  - pin 2
